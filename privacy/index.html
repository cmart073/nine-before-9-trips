Privacy Policy

How Nine Before 9 Trips collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

When you submit a trip planning inquiry through our contact forms, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Trip details (destination, group size, dates)
  • Any additional information you choose to provide

We do not collect payment information. We do not sell or share your information with third parties except as necessary to respond to your inquiry (for example, connecting you with a travel partner).

How We Use Your Information

Information you provide through trip planning forms is used solely to respond to your inquiry and connect you with appropriate travel resources. We may follow up via email to discuss your trip or provide additional information.

We do not use your information for marketing purposes unless you explicitly opt in. We do not share your email address with marketing lists or third-party advertisers.

Cookies and Analytics

This site may use standard web analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site. These tools collect anonymous information such as pages viewed, time spent on site, and referring sources. This data helps improve the site but does not identify individual users.

We do not use invasive tracking technologies or sell behavioral data to third parties.

Third-Party Links

This site contains links to third-party websites (hotels, booking platforms, airlines). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you provide through our contact forms. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we have collected about you. Contact us at [email protected] with any such requests.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
