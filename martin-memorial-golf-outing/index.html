Jeff Martin Memorial Golf Outing

A yearly gathering to remember Jeff — and spend a day doing what he loved: being with his people, outdoors, and competing just enough to laugh about it later.

About the outing

This outing is our way of keeping Jeff's name, stories, and spirit in the middle of the family calendar every year.

It's competitive, but not serious. The goal is simple: show up, play hard enough to care, and spend the day together. We'll run a 4-person scramble with Modified Stableford scoring, plus a few house rules that make it fun for every skill level.

If you knew Jeff, you already know why we do this. If you didn't, you'll feel it by the end of the day.

Date & location

We aim to hold the outing around Jeff's birthday — April 26 — or the weekend before/after.
